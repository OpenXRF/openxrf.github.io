Democratizing environmental heavy element detection

OpenXRF is an initiative for developing open-source X-ray fluorescence (XRF) instrumentation. We want to enable citizen-science efforts for mapping heavy element contamination in our environment.

Mission: Low-cost instrumentation for all

Target: <$100 per XRF device
